Flagship Project: Gravity Runner

The story behind our gravity-inverting pixel platformer built with Canvas physics.

Told as a Team Story

It started during a whiteboard session in Lab Komputer. We wanted to build a simple physics platformer, but with a twist: instead of jumping, the player inverts gravity to walk on the ceiling.

The Collaboration: Rian architected the rendering pipeline and hitboxes, while Jessica designed the neon sprite coordinates. Chris hooked up the Web Audio oscillator logic to synthesis retro collision noise.

We struggled with lag during rapid gravity inversions. After two weeks of playtests and performance refactorings, we replaced standard DOM components with a fast double-buffered canvas draw loop. Today, it runs smoothly at 60 FPS on any laptop!
