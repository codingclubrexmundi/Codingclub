Flagship Project: LavaLamp

The story behind our glowing CSS blobs and temperature sandbox simulator.

Told as a Team Story

It started during a whiteboard session in Lab Komputer. We wanted to build a lava lamp simulator in the browser using SVG gooey filters and vanilla CSS keyframe blobs.

The Collaboration: Rian wrote the HTML canvas particle physics logic, while Jessica designed the neon gradient CSS variables. Chris hooked up the slider widgets to control the temperature and rising speed on the fly.

We spent hours getting the gooey blur filter to merge particles smoothly without lagging the viewport. After three nights of group debugging and pairing sessions, we solved the rendering repaints. Today, it's one of our most popular sandboxes!

Tech Stack:
HTML5 Canvas CSS Animations SVG gooey filter Vanilla JS
